# Funghi Pizza

Ingredients:

- Pizza dough
- Tomato sauce
- Mozzarella
- Mushrooms
- Garlic
- Olive oil
- Oregano

Instructions:

2. Fry mushrooms with garlic
3. Spread sauce on dough
